Topographic Map

A laser-cut topographical map of the Saanich Peninsula.

From CAD to physical product

Cut from plywood on my school's glowforge laser printer, this map shows the physical geography of the Saanich Peninsula and sourrounding islands. The project had three stages:

  1. CAD: Using Google Earth and Government of Canada survey data, I traced the relevant contours in Vectorworks and produced renders to visualize my design.
  2. Production: The glowforge cut the contour lines in plywood, taking about an hour.
  3. Assembly: The layers of wood were stacked and glued together, and painted blue (in the case of the water) or waxed (land).

Three copies have been made so far.
