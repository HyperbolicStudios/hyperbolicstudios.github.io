Victoria LRT

Identifying future LRT routes between Downtown and UVic

See our presentation

We analyzed the feasibility of implementing a light rail transit (LRT) system in the Greater Victoria Area. We used ArcGIS spatial analyst tools to determine the most efficient, cost-effective route that maximizes the total ridership of the LRT. >We analyzed 3 existing bus routes (routes 4, 7, and 15), with three criteria:

  • Population served
  • Typical existing road width
  • Route length

> Our analysis concluded that Route 15 would be the most practical route to construct an LRT line on. Route 15 had the highest serviced population (36921 people) and the 2nd highest % on a major road (46.4%), and although it has a high total length (26594m), we believe the amount of population coverage and % on major roads should make the construction of this LRT route viable.
