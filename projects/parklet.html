Parklet

Putting water at the heart of concrete cities.

Bring back water to our cities.

In a world of concrete and glass, we need urban spaces that provide a more meaningful connection to the planet. Water, Stone, Wood, and Greens come together in harmony to provide a fulfilling, spiritual sense of peace. > What is a parklet? A parklet is a parking space converted to an urban space: where people can meet, sit, and relax.

Clean lines and an uncluttered use of space define this parklet.
