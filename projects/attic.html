Attic Reno

Contest Submission


View the full drawing set.
This design contest was to turn an old attic into a 2 bedroom, 2 bathroom appartment in Milan. This design features a minimalist, modern, and expansive living room that is continuous with the open-plan kitchen. Two design options were studied: Option 1 features a climate-controlled pergola that provides a pleasant space to eat during all seasons; Option 2 has the dining table inside.

West Coast


A 2x2 pool rests on the far end of the balcony, along with a fire pit/BBQ, and a tasteful collection of modern chairs and tables to relax outside. The patio is sorrounded by 70cm planters around the side to create privacy and is covered by polished concrete pavers.

Efficient, Clever, Spacious


While the multiple space constraints were challenging, we have created a design that provides space for an extended family, tenants, and future renovation, while maintaining a spacious, comfortable, and well-lit house that does not feel tight or cramp.

Extended Family Space


The two bedrooms each have a bathroom, and a dressing room that takes advantage of the sloping roof is also included. New floor-to-ceiling windows for the living room allow ample sunlight to flow into the kitchen, living room, and master bedroom.
