Keyboard

Slow your typing down.


Sometimes, we need a break. Take more time to write your urgent words, and instead take in the beauty of the tools around you. Using the not-original QWERTY layout, this brilliant keyboard slows your typing down to allow you to relax and enjoy typing for as long as possible.

Whatever you're writing isn't important. Slow it down.

Easy to make, hard to use.


The default blank texture and imported Generic Vectorworks Texture Chrome melds together to create a sleek, elegant keyboard.
Resemblance to imac keyboard not purely coincidental. (I just modeled the keyboard that's in front of me!)
