About Me

Quick facts:

  • Currently a third year civil engineering student at UVic in Victoria, BC.
  • Recently employed by PCL Construction in Calgary as an Estimating Student, and with BC Transit as transit planning co-op student.
  • Passionate about CAD, civil infrastructure, and transit.

    • See some of my recent work.

I'm passionate about design and it's potential to change the world.

I’ve taken four courses on Drafting and Design, and have been enthralled. I love imagining a design to make people’s lives better and bringing it to life through CAD. I’ve designed houses, interior renovations and urban settings such as a parklet, as part of my second year of Drafting. While I’m relatively new with four years of experience with Vectorworks in school, I’m a strong student who’s shown a lot a potential using it and I’m a fast learner who loves buildings and design.

Learn more about myself.

Experienced with CAD

My favorite CAD program is Vectorworks, and I do most of my renderings with TwinMotion and Lumion to visualize my designs with crystal-clear realism. I'm also somewhat proficient in Revit, for civil engineering work, and Fusion360, for mechanical and 3D printing projects.
